One-Year Cycle
Seven phases.
Full-year guidance.
Day 0–10
Germination
Daily water, warmth, no fertiliser yet
Day 10–30
Seedling
Gentle water, first organic fertiliser at day 15
Day 30–60
Vegetative
Regular water, nitrogen, weed removal, pest check
Day 60–75
Flowering
Reduce nitrogen, add potassium, stake if needed
Day 75–85
Fruiting
Minimal water, pest vigilance, sun check
Day 85–90
Pre-harvest
Stop fertiliser, reduce water, inspect readiness
Day 90–365
Post-cycle
Summary, option to replant, soil recovery guide
